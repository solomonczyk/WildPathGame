# Financial Boundaries

Currency: USD equivalent. Amounts are maximum planning caps, not spending commitments.

## Stage Budgets

See [Budget Matrix](data/budget-matrix.json).

## Maximum Budget

Maximum budget to reach first paid pilot: 4,500 USD equivalent.

Budget categories:

- AI API and image generation: 350.
- Infrastructure and tools: 250.
- Design and testing: 900.
- Marketing experiments: 1,200.
- Support and customer calls: 450.
- Legal and safety review: 700.
- Contingency reserve: 650.

## AI API Budget

AI use is allowed for ideation, translation drafts, image generation, test data, and code assistance. AI may not be the final authority for survival, medical, legal, or marketing claims.

Hard cap before paid pilot: 350 USD equivalent.

## Infrastructure Budget

Use low-cost static hosting and browser storage until payment and account needs are proven. Do not add a database before a requirement forces it.

Hard cap before paid pilot: 250 USD equivalent.

## Design and Testing Budget

Spend on images, UX testing incentives, and device checks. Priority is clarity over visual spectacle.

Hard cap before paid pilot: 900 USD equivalent.

## Marketing Budget

Organic content first. Paid marketing starts only after [GTM ad launch criteria](gtm-validation.md#ad-launch-criteria).

Hard cap before paid pilot: 1,200 USD equivalent.

## Support Budget

Support is manual during paid pilot. Track time per user. If support exceeds 20 minutes per paid user, onboarding must be fixed before scaling.

Hard cap before paid pilot: 450 USD equivalent.

## Legal Review Budget

Use legal or qualified review for disclaimers, payment terms, and safety-sensitive public claims before paid pilot.

Hard cap before paid pilot: 700 USD equivalent.

## Contingency Reserve

Reserve is 650 and may be used only by owner decision recorded in [Decision Log](data/decision-log.json).

## Budget Overrun Rules

- Up to 10% over stage planned budget: owner records reason.
- 10-25% over: change request required.
- More than 25% over: stage HOLD until scope is cut or budget is re-approved.
- Any spend that accelerates production without learning or payment evidence is blocked.
