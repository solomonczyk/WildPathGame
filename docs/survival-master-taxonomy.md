# Survival Master Taxonomy

Status: `DRAFT_MASTER_TAXONOMY`.
Owner: Taras.
Purpose: задать полный рабочий список локаций, угроз и ситуационных семейств, на основе которого строится survival-энциклопедия и игровые эпизоды WildPathGame.

Этот документ расширяет начальный список локаций и угроз. Он не является финальной инструкцией по выживанию. Это карта контента: что нужно покрыть, как классифицировать, какие разделы требуют официальных источников и человеческого review.

Initial project content catalog: `152` scenario seeds. This number is the sum of all concrete `Scenes` listed under the 20 location families below. These seeds are the starting library for future authored episodes, encyclopedia entries, item cards, and debrief lessons. They are not all release-ready playable episodes.

## 1. Как читать этот список

Список делится на три слоя:

- `Location Families`: где происходит ситуация.
- `Threat Families`: что угрожает человеку.
- `Situation Families`: какой тип решения должен принять игрок.

Игровой эпизод должен собираться как комбинация:

```text
location + threat + player state + available items + objective + consequence
```

Например:

```text
High-rise apartment + power outage + darkness + limited time + evacuation packing
Forest trail + lost orientation + rain + low phone battery + stay-or-move decision
Industrial edge + chemical cloud + official alert + family pressure + shelter-in-place
```

## 4. Situation Families

### 4.1 Immediate Evacuation

Player question:

- What do I take, what do I leave, and when do I stop packing?

Use for:

- apartment fire risk;
- official evacuation order;
- wildfire route closure;
- chemical evacuation;
- flood evacuation;
- aircraft cabin evacuation.

### 4.2 Shelter-in-Place

Player question:

- Which room/place is safer, and what should I seal, monitor, or avoid?

Use for:

- chemical cloud;
- radiation alert;
- severe storm;
- smoke/air quality;
- civil disruption;
- winter storm indoors.

### 4.3 Stay or Move

Player question:

- Is movement safer than waiting?

Use for:

- lost forest;
- broken car;
- injured hiker;
- desert heat;
- snowstorm;
- post-crash remote site.

### 4.4 Find Water / Protect Water

Player question:

- What water is usable, what is unsafe, and how much should be carried?

Use for:

- apartment evacuation;
- road delay;
- desert;
- forest;
- jungle;
- ocean raft;
- flood zone.

### 4.5 Manage Temperature

Player question:

- How do I reduce heat/cold stress with available items and behavior?

Use for:

- snow;
- cold rain forest;
- mountain wind;
- urban heating outage;
- desert heat;
- humid jungle.

### 4.6 Navigate and Signal

Player question:

- How do I become easier to find and avoid becoming more lost?

Use for:

- forest;
- mountain;
- jungle;
- snow whiteout;
- ocean;
- post-crash.

### 4.7 Treat or Stabilize Injury

Player question:

- What immediate safe action reduces risk without pretending to replace medical care?

Use for:

- stairwell fall;
- road crash;
- cut in floodwater;
- mountain sprain;
- aircraft crash;
- industrial injury.

### 4.8 Avoid Secondary Hazards

Player question:

- What danger appears after the first event?

Use for:

- earthquake aftershock/fire/gas leak;
- flood contamination/electricity;
- wildfire smoke/route closure;
- hurricane debris/floodwater;
- industrial spill exposure;
- winter carbon monoxide risk, only with reviewed source guidance.

### 4.9 Resource Triage

Player question:

- Which limited supplies matter first?

Use for:

- shop/mall;
- shelter;
- vehicle kit;
- family go-bag;
- community water point;
- long outage.

### 4.10 Communication Failure

Player question:

- What plan works when phone/internet/payment fails?

Use for:

- city outage;
- road;
- forest;
- airport disruption;
- family separation;
- industrial alert.

### 4.11 Crowd and Social Pressure

Player question:

- How do I stay calm, visible, fair, and non-confrontational?

Use for:

- evacuation queue;
- transit shutdown;
- shelter intake;
- shop scarcity;
- event venue incident;
- family disagreement under stress.

### 4.12 Long-Duration Survival

Player question:

- How do I preserve health and decision quality over days?

Use for:

- long outage;
- remote vehicle breakdown;
- ocean raft;
- snow isolation;
- post-disaster shelter;
- remote settlement disruption.

- poisoning;

- frostbite;
- heat stroke;
- contaminated water;
- infectious disease;
- chemical exposure;

- wildfire evacuation;
- floodwater hazards;
- structural collapse;
- crowd crush;
- self-rescue stories involving extreme injury.
