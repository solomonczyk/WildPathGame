# Learning and Domain Methodology

## Subject Methodology

WildPathGame teaches practical prioritization, not exhaustive survival mastery. The learning model is:

1. Present a realistic constrained situation.
2. Let the player act before instruction.
3. Show consequence.
4. Explain the underlying real-world principle.
5. Repeat across varied contexts.

## Progression

Progression moves from immediate bodily needs to environmental and social complexity:

1. Evacuation packing.
2. Warmth and fire basics.
3. Water safety.
4. Shelter and exposure.
5. Injury management.
6. Human risk and cooperation.

## Assessment

Each episode assesses:

- essential recognition;
- avoidance of plausible traps;
- weight and time tradeoff;
- ability to explain why an item matters.

Mastery is not a score alone. A learner demonstrates mastery when they can explain the principle after play.

## Quality Criteria

Learning content must be:

- actionable but not reckless;
- specific about context;
- clear about uncertainty;
- aligned with recognized safety principles;
- reviewed before paid pilot.

## Content Rules

- No claim of guaranteed survival.
- No medical certification language.
- No weaponized framing for ordinary tools.
- No instructions that encourage illegal trapping, hunting, trespass, or unsafe fire use without context and warnings.
- Every dangerous domain claim needs human review.

## Human Review

Before paid pilot:

- survival/outdoor reviewer checks field realism;
- medical reviewer or first-aid qualified reviewer checks injury content;
- product owner checks tone and promise.

## Source of Truth

The game source of truth is the canonical episode data in `src/lib/interactiveEpisodes.js`, the [Scenario and Domain Canon](scenario-domain-canon.md), and this documentation package. External videos or AI outputs are references, not authority.

## Scenario Domain Control

New survival domains are not added directly to gameplay because they sound exciting. Location families, hazard families, source hierarchy, review status values, and mode-scaling rules are governed by the [Scenario and Domain Canon](scenario-domain-canon.md).

Before first release, adding a new location family, hazard family, or safety-sensitive disaster domain requires a recorded change request. Implementing radiation, chemical, severe weather, maritime, aviation, or injury-heavy content also requires official-source review and human domain review before it can be treated as approved educational content.

## Authority Boundaries

The game can teach prioritization and awareness. It cannot certify competence, replace medical care, replace local legal knowledge, or guarantee correct action in every emergency.

## Forbidden Shortcuts

- Adding content because it sounds dramatic.
- Making items useful without explaining why.
- Marking AI-generated survival claims as accepted without review.
- Treating technical completion as learning completion.
- Expanding content library before first six episodes meet quality criteria.
