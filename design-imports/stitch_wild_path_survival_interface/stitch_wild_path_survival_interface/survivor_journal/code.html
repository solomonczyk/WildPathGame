Episode 1: The Outbreak

Date: Unknown | Loc: Urban Sector 4

edit_note

Field Notes

It happened fast. The sirens started at 0400 hours. By sunrise, the streets were blocked. I managed to secure the apartment, but supplies are severely limited.

The power grid is failing intermittently. I need to prioritize finding a stable light source that won't attract unwanted attention. Flashlights are good, but batteries will be an issue soon.

Saw movement in the alleyway. They aren't moving like regular people. Erratic. Unpredictable. Best to avoid line of sight completely.

Survival Protocol Alpha

The Rule of Threes

You can survive 3 minutes without oxygen, 3 hours without shelter in extreme environments, 3 days without water, and 3 weeks without food. Prioritize accordingly.
